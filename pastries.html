Pastries

Tres Leches

A celebration of tradition, this classic Tres Leches is the ultimate comfort dessert. A light airy sponge is soaked to perfection in a sweet trio of condensed milk, evaporated milk, and heavy cream. Topped with a cloud of pillowy whipped cream and a dusting of cinnamon, it delivers a melt-in-your-mouth experience that is incredibly moist and refreshing.

Gata

The centerpiece of celebration, this large traditional Gata features a soft, slightly sweet exterior that is beautifully scored with decorative patterns. The interior holds a dense, buttery khoriz filling made from flour, sugar, and butter, delivering a rich, satisfying sweetness that is perfect when served with coffee or tea.

Walnut Cake

A study in texture and flavor, this dense walnut cake is left completely unfrosted to showcase its deep golden color and the exposed nuts. It is served with a light maple-caramel drizzle and a scattering of candied walnuts, creating an elegant presentation that focuses entirely on the moist crumb and aromatic sweetness.
