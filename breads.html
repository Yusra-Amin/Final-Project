Breads

Sourdough Bread

This is the definitive artisan loaf: a perfectly rounded Boule achieved through skilled shaping and slow fermentation. The deep, tangy sourdough flavor is sealed inside a thick, rustic crust that crackles when sliced, revealing an airy, uneven crumb. It makes a stunning centerpiece for any table.

Raisin Bread

This traditional sweet bread is the perfect comforting treat. Our enriched dough is warmly spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg, featuring plump, sweet raisins studded throughout. Baked to a beautiful golden brown, it has a wonderfully tender and soft crumb and is delicious served toasted with butter for breakfast or brunch.

French Baguette

This iconic, slender loaf is the definition of French simplicity and excellence. Baked to a deep golden hue, the thin, shattery crust is covered in delicate blisters, providing a satisfying crackle with every bite. The crisp exterior gives way to a creamy white, airy interior perfect for sandwiches or dipping.
