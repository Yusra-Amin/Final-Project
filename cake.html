Cakes

Wedding Cake

A timeless masterpiece, this four-tier vanilla bean cake is wrapped in smooth white fondant and draped with cascading fresh roses. Inside, layers of light, airy sponge meet a tart raspberry preserve and silky Swiss meringue buttercream for a bite that is as elegant as it is delicious.

Caramel Cake

Embracing warm and comforting flavors, this golden caramel cake is studded with toasted pecans for a delightful crunch. It is layered with a light cinnamon-spiced cream cheese frosting and finished with a semi-naked scrape, creating a rustic look that tastes like a cozy embrace.

Napoleon Cake

A sophisticated tribute to French patisserie, this classic Napoleon cake is a masterpiece of texture. It features countless layers of shatteringly crisp, buttery puff pastry alternating with cool, velvety vanilla bean crème pâtissière. Topped with a traditional marbled fondant glaze, it offers a delicate crunch and a creamy finish in every bite.
